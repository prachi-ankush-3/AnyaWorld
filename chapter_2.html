Chapter 2

A Family Begins to Grow

The Forgers begin their new life together as Anya prepares for Eden Academy.

Morning sunlight gently slipped through the curtains, filling the little apartment with a warm golden glow.

For the very first time in many years, Anya woke up with a smile.

She wasn't in an orphanage anymore.

She had a home.

She stretched her tiny arms, jumped out of bed, and hurried toward the kitchen where a delicious smell drifted through the apartment.

Loid was already preparing breakfast.

Although he appeared calm as always, his thoughts were racing.

"Operation Strix has officially begun. First, I need to prepare Anya for the entrance exam."

Anya quietly giggled.

"Papa always thinks so seriously."

Trying not to reveal that she could hear every thought, she simply smiled and climbed onto her chair.

"Good morning, Papa!"

Loid smiled back.

"Good morning, Anya."

For a brief moment, the apartment felt less like a fake home...

...and more like a real family.

After breakfast, Loid explained that Anya would soon have to attend one of the finest schools in the country.

Eden Academy.

It was famous for its elegant uniforms, brilliant students, and strict entrance examinations.

Anya listened carefully.

Inside her head, however, she imagined something completely different.

Huge castles...

Secret spy classrooms...

Students battling villains...

And herself becoming the greatest secret agent ever.

She couldn't stop smiling.

Loid handed her several practice books.

"Let's begin studying."

Anya opened the first page.

History...

Mathematics...

Grammar...

Her smile slowly disappeared.

"This is... difficult."

Loid patiently explained every question.

Anya tried her best, but every few minutes she became distracted by something else.

Sometimes she stared out the window.

Sometimes she imagined herself chasing villains across rooftops.

Sometimes she simply wondered what Loid was thinking.

Every time she secretly read his mind...

She discovered that he cared far more about her success than he allowed himself to admit.

Days passed.

Morning after morning, they studied together.

Although Anya struggled with difficult lessons, she never gave up.

Not because she loved studying.

But because she wanted Loid to be proud of her.

"She's improving little by little."

Anya secretly smiled.

"Papa praised me!"

One afternoon, Loid suddenly stopped reading.

A new thought crossed his mind.

"A normal family should visit the tailor."

The entrance interview required proper clothing.

Together, they walked through busy streets filled with cheerful shoppers.

Everything amazed Anya.

Colorful toys.

Sweet shops.

Ice cream stands.

Dogs running happily beside their owners.

She had never experienced such an ordinary day before.

For someone who had spent most of her childhood moving from one family to another...

Even a simple walk felt magical.

As they returned home carrying neatly folded clothes, Anya looked up at Loid.

Without thinking, she gently reached for his hand.

Loid looked surprised for only a second...

Then quietly held her hand in return.

Neither of them said a word.

But both of them smiled.

That evening, after dinner, Anya sat beside the window watching the stars appear one by one.

She thought about everything that had happened.

Only a few days ago...

She had been alone.

Now she had someone who cooked breakfast for her.

Someone who helped her study.

Someone who walked beside her and held her hand.

She hugged her favorite stuffed toy and whispered softly,

"Maybe... this is what having a family feels like."

In the next room, Loid quietly finished writing his mission report.

Before closing the notebook, he paused.

His thoughts drifted away from Operation Strix for just a moment.

Instead, he looked toward Anya's room.

A small smile appeared on his face.

Perhaps...

This family wasn't becoming important only for the mission.

Perhaps...

It was becoming important to his heart.

🌸 End of Chapter 2

As the entrance exam drew closer, Anya's biggest challenge was still waiting for her.

Little did she know...

The next step toward becoming a true member of the Forger family would change all of their lives forever.
