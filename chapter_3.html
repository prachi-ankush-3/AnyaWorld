Chapter 3

The Search for a Mother

Loid's mission takes a new turn as the Forgers begin searching for someone to complete their unusual family.

Several days passed peacefully.

Every morning, Loid and Anya studied together.

Every afternoon, they practiced interview questions for Eden Academy.

Little by little...

Their fake family began to feel surprisingly real.

But one evening...

Loid sat alone at his desk, quietly reviewing Operation Strix.

His expression suddenly became serious.

"The interview requires both parents."

He slowly closed the file.

"Without a wife... the mission cannot continue."

Anya quietly listened from behind the door.

Her eyes grew wide.

"Papa needs... a mama?"

She had never really thought about it before.

The apartment already felt warm and comfortable.

But perhaps...

It still wasn't complete.

The next morning, Loid gently explained everything.

"Anya," he said softly,

"We need to find someone who can pretend to be your mother."

Anya blinked.

Then suddenly smiled brightly.

"Yay! Mama!"

Inside, however, Loid remained focused.

"This is only for the mission."

Anya secretly laughed.

"Papa is lying again."

Loid searched everywhere.

He considered coworkers.

Old acquaintances.

Even professional actors.

None of them were suitable.

Some asked too many questions.

Some politely refused.

Others simply didn't fit the role.

Anya carefully watched every meeting.

Sometimes she secretly read people's thoughts.

"Too scary."
"Too boring."
"Definitely not Mama."

Every unsuccessful meeting made Loid more worried.

Time was quickly running out.

That afternoon...

They stopped outside a small tailor shop.

While Loid checked the interview schedule, Anya noticed someone across the street.

A beautiful young woman with long black hair quietly walked past carrying several shopping bags.

Her gentle smile immediately caught Anya's attention.

"She's pretty..."

Without warning...

Anya suddenly heard something unexpected inside the woman's mind.

"I hope I can find a fake boyfriend before the party..."

Anya gasped.

"She also has a secret!"

Without wasting another second, Anya grabbed Loid's hand.

"Papa! Look!"

Before Loid could stop her, Anya ran happily toward the mysterious young woman.

"Hello!" she said with a bright smile.

The woman looked surprised for a moment before smiling warmly.

"Oh... hello there."

Anya pointed proudly toward Loid.

"My papa needs a wife!"

Loid nearly froze in embarrassment.

"Anya!"

His face turned bright red while the woman couldn't help laughing.

"My name is Yor Briar," she introduced herself politely.

Loid quickly bowed.

"I'm Loid Forger. It's a pleasure to meet you."

As they talked, both of them slowly realized something surprising.

Loid needed a wife for the Eden Academy interview.

Yor needed someone to pretend to be her boyfriend for an upcoming party.

Perhaps...

They could help each other.

The three spent the afternoon walking through the city together.

Anya happily skipped between them, secretly reading both of their thoughts.

"These two are perfect together!"

Neither Loid nor Yor noticed Anya's excited smile.

As the sun slowly disappeared beyond the rooftops, the three walked together beneath the glowing orange sky.

To everyone around them, they looked like an ordinary family enjoying a peaceful evening.

But each of them carried an extraordinary secret.

Loid was a legendary spy.

Yor was a highly skilled assassin.

Anya could read people's minds.

None of them knew the others' greatest secret.

Yet somehow...

Fate had brought them together.

Perhaps this unusual family was meant to be.

🌸 End of Chapter 3

A new member was about to join the Forger family.

But pretending to be the perfect family would soon become much more difficult than anyone expected.

Their greatest adventure was only just beginning.
